## Change log (material)

1. **New central thesis**: single-boundary claim-promotion → dual Discovery
   Plane / Trust Plane, institutionally separated (Principle 1). The Trust Plane
   *is* the preserved v1 architecture.
2. **Two implemented substrates integrated as contracts**, not duplicated:
   MIRADOR (Contract 6.1) and ProofContext (Contract 7.1), cited by Zenodo DOI,
   with honest maturity in Table 1.
3. **Goal graph → typed research hypergraph**; object type made orthogonal to
   epistemic state (Principle 5); 15 object types, 11 epistemic states.
4. **Discovery Plane added**: 13 machine-actionable operators, each with a
   validation obligation and a predictable failure mode; two research loops.
5. **Congress enlarged**: correspondence, novelty/priority, retrieval-provenance,
   assumption/scope, checker-independence, and integration/invalidation lenses;
   veto is now a structured object.
6. **Validation replaced**: the two-stage plan with USD budgets ($20k / $320k)
   is **removed**; superseded by the R0–R9 readiness ladder and the three-track
   program. The engine cannot self-declare R9.
7. **Millennium framing corrected**: no title/abstract promise of a solution; it
   is a gated stress test whose "solution" is defined outside the engine.
8. **Pilot honesty**: Bang is a retrospective vertical slice; MIRADOR /
   ProofContext / Discovery operators were **not** implemented during it, and the
   text says so.
9. **Maturity ledger + threat model** added so no unbuilt mechanism reads as
   built; no row claims external validation.
10. **Related work**: capability-and-authority matrix; a companion primary-source
    audit (`audits/RELATED_WORK_MATRIX.md`) verifies every 2026 citation.
